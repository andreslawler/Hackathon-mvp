# Customer RFI Extract — Integrated Core Solution (Mission Critical Networks)

A leading GCC operator, acting as prime for a national mission-critical end customer, has issued an RFI for a dedicated private 4G/5G core, and requests an indicative budgetary quotation within seven working days.

## Stated requirements

### Scope
- A dedicated, logically isolated private 4G/5G dual-mode core, reusing the operator's existing RAN and spectrum.
- Cloud-native, deployed on the customer's premises.
- Geo-redundant deployment across two sites, with 99.99% availability.

### Capacity (over the contract horizon)
- Up to approximately 20,000 provisioned and simultaneously active users.
- Voice service (VoLTE / IMS) for up to approximately 5,000 active users.
- At least 40 Gbps active core throughput, with a defined scaling path.
- A service mix spanning mission-critical communications (push-to-talk and push-to-video), native voice, and private data and IoT.

### Services
- Solution design, deployment, integration, testing, acceptance, and go-live support.

### Commercial
- The customer is open to a multi-year commitment if it improves the commercial proposition.

## Requested response
A budgetary quotation package within seven working days: a pricing schedule, a solution description, and a statement of compliance to the requirements above.
